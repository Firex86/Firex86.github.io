Arma 3

Released: 2013
Type: Tactical Milsim
Playtime: 1200+ hrs

Gameplay & Added Content

Games gameplay differs greatly from your typical 1st person shooters as Arma 3 focuses on milsim and realism. A couple of examples are the physics engine witch calculates penetration value for bullets etc. and the size of the maps which can reach up to 120 squarekilometres

After the 2013 release of the game, the developer team has been hard at work creating additional content that is being released yearly.

Community

Arma 3 has around 13 thousand active players monthly. While that doesn't sound that impressive you have to remember that while the community is small when compared to another triple-A game, it does have a loyal fan base that keeps on going even if the game is already 8 years old.

Community keeps on creating additional content with user made mods and missions. Few note worthy mentions would be RHS (Russian and US equipment), TFAR (Radio mod) and ACE (interaction tool)
