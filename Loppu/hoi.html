Hearts Of Iron 4

Released: 2016
Type: Grand Strategy
Playtime: 300+ hrs

Gameplay

Fourth installation in the Hearts of Iron series doesn't leave you cold. Perhaps the best grand strategy focused on World War 2. You can play as any nation during ww2 and shape the history to your liking.

There is no set end objective so you can create any scenario you wish to accomplish. These can wary from historical campaign as United States or world conquest as Mexico.

Minor Details

Details in the game are quite staggering. Different terrain types give you different penalties or bonuses depending you division templates and each battalition within the division has different stats.

This can seem as difficult to a new players, but once you get some hours in the game you start to understand the core mechanics of the game.
