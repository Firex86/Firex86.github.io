War Thunder

Released: 2013
Type: Military MMO
Playtime: 1360+ hrs

Gameplay

Game which does what no other game does. Combining airforces, groundforces and naval vehicle is no easy chore, but Gaijin has done it. From the original three countries which were avaivable at launch the developers have added more countries and more vehicle on a monthly basis.

As of today the game has over 1900 different vehicle that you can play with. These differ from early 1930s biplanes to early 2000s mainbattle tanks.

Issues With The Game

Unfortunately creating such a multilayered game there will be some issues that are hard to fix. Perhaps the most notable is the difficulty with balancing new and old vehicles. Adding more and more advanced vehicles will be far more powerfull than the older counterparts.

This is most notable with advanced antiair vehicles and planes. Currently there is no counter to advanced anti air vehicle which leads to planes being unable to defend against missile systems etc.
